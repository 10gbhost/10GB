About 10G Energy Solutions

Pioneering Nigeria's transition to sustainable, reliable, and intelligent energy systems

Who We Are

10G Energy Solutions is Nigeria's premier provider of renewable energy and smart home technology. Founded with a vision to revolutionize how Nigerians access and consume energy, we have grown to become the trusted partner for hundreds of homes, businesses, and institutions across the nation.

Our name, "10G", symbolizes our commitment to being ten generations ahead in energy innovation, sustainability, and customer service. We don't just install solar panels—we deliver comprehensive energy ecosystems that transform lives and empower communities.

With a team of certified engineers, energy consultants, and smart home specialists, we bring international standards of excellence to every project while maintaining deep roots in understanding Nigeria's unique energy challenges and opportunities.
