Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does installation take?

Residential installations typically take 3-5 days. Commercial projects vary based on system size, usually 1-3 weeks.

Do you offer financing?

Yes! We provide flexible payment plans and installment options to make solar energy accessible to more Nigerians.

What's included in the warranty?

Solar panels: 25 years performance warranty. Inverters: 5-10 years. Batteries: 5-10 years. Installation workmanship: 2 years.

Do you provide maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance packages including regular system checks, cleaning, and 24/7 technical support.

Can I expand my system later?

Absolutely! Our modular systems are designed for easy expansion as your energy needs grow.

Which areas do you service?

We operate across all 36 states in Nigeria, with major presence in Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, and other major cities.
