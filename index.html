Solar Work Calculator

Estimate your solar system requirements based on your energy needs

How to Use This Calculator

Understanding the Inputs

  • Load in Watts: Total power consumption of all appliances you want to run simultaneously (e.g., 2 ACs = 2000W, 5 lights = 500W, TV = 200W, fridge = 150W = Total 2850W)
  • Daily Usage Hours: Average number of hours per day you use these appliances (e.g., if you run your system for 8 hours daily, enter 8)

Understanding the Outputs

  • Inverter Size (kVA): The capacity of inverter needed to handle your load (includes 25% surge capacity for startup)
  • Battery Capacity (Ah): Required battery capacity at 48V to store energy for your needs (based on 50% depth of discharge)
  • Number of Solar Panels: Quantity of 550W solar panels needed (based on 5 peak sun hours in Nigeria)

Pro Tip: This calculator provides estimates based on average Nigerian conditions. For a precise system design tailored to your specific location and needs, contact our experts for a free consultation.
