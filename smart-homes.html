What is a Smart Home?

A smart home integrates connected devices and systems that can be controlled remotely via smartphone, tablet, or voice commands. From adjusting your air conditioning before you arrive home to monitoring security cameras from anywhere in the world, smart home technology puts complete control at your fingertips.

10G's smart home solutions seamlessly integrate with your solar energy system, allowing you to monitor energy production and consumption in real-time, automate energy-intensive appliances to run during peak solar hours, and maximize your savings.

Whether you're building a new home or upgrading an existing property, our modular smart home systems can be customized to meet your specific needs and expanded over time.
